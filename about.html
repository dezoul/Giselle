Giselle is a restaurant born from friendship and love, created by a group of friends with deep roots and experience in hospitality. More than just a place to eat, it reflects the soul of the Riviera — warm, vibrant, and timeless, like an endless summer. The menu is built around local, responsibly sourced ingredients, cooked with care, passion, and a touch of Mediterranean elegance. Every dish, every glass, every detail is meant to offer our guests the very best.

Located in the heart of Batu Bolong on Jalan Tanah Barak, just a few hundred meters from the beach, Giselle is where sunsets turn into nights, where flavors meet stories, and where the Riviera finds a home in Canggu.
